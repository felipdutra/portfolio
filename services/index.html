How
Share what you’ve got or just your idea.
You can send over your logo, website, app screenshots, pitch deck, or even just a rough concept. Whether we’re starting from scratch or improving something existing, we’ll take it from there.
Discovery and direction
We’ll take time to understand your audience, your goals, and what might be standing in the way. From there we define the best path. Sometimes it’s a full redesign, other times it’s building something brand new.
Design and iteration
We’ll create 1 to 3 initial directions, refine them with your feedback, and prepare all final files along with any additional variations or assets you might need.
Final delivery
If you love it, awesome! And if you’re okay with it, we might even feature the work on our socials. If you’d like to tweak anything, no worries. We’ll revise based on the number of rounds in your package.
Yours to use however you like
Once it’s all approved, the design is fully yours. You’ll have full commercial and personal rights to use it however you need.
