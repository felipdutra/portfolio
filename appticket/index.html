Overview

Principal Product Designer, and UI Front-end Developer. AppTicket is a sales platform and social strategies for events. More than 20.000 event producers across Brazil use AppTicket to sell tickets and registrations online for their events. Business meetings, music and sports events, parties, and so on.

Roles

Product Design, UI Front-end, Product Strategy, Research, Prototyping, User flows, Design System, Branding, Pitch Decks.

Goals

  • Plan, design, and deliver continuous improvements and new features for event organizers and online ticket buyers, always focused on solving core user pain points.
  • Define the team's vision, mission, and values to establish a strong foundation and shared purpose.
  • Develop a short- and medium-term strategy aligned with business objectives, technological opportunities, and user needs.
  • Create a prioritized feature backlog to ensure a clear roadmap for development.
  • Analyze results and outline next steps to guide future iterations and improvements.

Roles

  • Led the end-to-end product design process, aligning business goals with the real needs of event organizers and ticket buyers.
  • Drove product strategy in close collaboration, translating complex challenges into clear, actionable design solutions that supported Appticket’s mission and vision.
  • Designed scalable, accessible, and user-centered interfaces, and was also responsible for front-end development—ensuring pixel-perfect execution and seamless integration between design and code.
  • Advocated for design best practices and fostered a culture of continuous improvement.
  • Utilized a modern tech stack—Vue.js, Sass, and Figma—to bridge the gap between design and development and deliver cohesive, high-quality experiences.

Revamp complex dashboard

Problems & Goals

  • Fragmented experience: Event producers struggled with a scattered interface that didn’t reflect the real complexity of managing a live event.
  • Lack of control: Users lacked visibility over key metrics such as ticket sales, check-ins, promoter performance, and financial insights.
  • Unclear data visualization: Important data was hard to access and understand, making day-to-day decision-making slow and inefficient.
  • Need for full autonomy: One of the main goals was to give producers complete control over all aspects of their events—without needing external help.

Methodology

  • Redesigned the entire UI with a focus on visual clarity, responsive layouts, and a seamless experience across complex dashboard views.
  • Built a scalable design system from scratch, standardizing components and interactions for visual consistency and faster development.
  • Implemented the full front-end—from layout to microinteractions—ensuring a high-performance, intuitive, and polished user interface.

Impact & Results

  • 15–20% faster access to key tools and information, improving daily workflow efficiency.
  • Users felt more in control during live events, especially with real-time sales and check-in tracking.
  • Increased confidence and autonomy; producers reported needing less support for routine tasks.
  • Successfully tested across events from 200 to 10,000+ attendees, with stable performance.
  • Support team noted a visible drop in dashboard-related questions, especially around navigation.

Website redesign

Problems & Goals

  • Transform Appticket into an event catalog that appeals to both consumers and event producers.
  • Increase event visibility and drive more traffic to featured events.
  • Develop landing pages for specific event categories to enhance sales efforts and highlight Appticket's large-scale event offering.
  • Reinforce Appticket's credibility as a major ticketing platform.

Methodology

  • Designed intuitive user flows and created responsive, visually appealing UI to enhance event discovery and conversion.
  • Developed landing pages for various event categories, integrating persuasive messaging and clear functionality.
  • Implemented Vue.js and front-end technologies to ensure a dynamic and responsive website experience.

Impact & Results

  • Traffic to featured events grew by 35%, and average session duration increased by 20% due to improved navigation and design.
  • Conversion rates on landing pages improved by 25%, with specific event category pages seeing a 30% increase in visits.
  • 85% of users described the platform as trustworthy, contributing to brand recognition in key markets.
  • Bounce rates dropped by 15%, and overall site conversions increased by 9%, thanks to a smoother and more interactive user experience.
