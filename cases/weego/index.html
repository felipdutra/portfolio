Weego
2019 - 2022 · More about Weego ↗
Founding Team, Partner, Sr. Product Designer, and UI Front-end Developer. Weego is a B2B SaaS platform focused on leadership development through personalized learning experiences. Companies across Brazil use Weego to support leaders by helping them grow through microlearning, climate surveys, 360º assessments, daily reflections, and team development tools.
Roles
Product Design, UI Front-end, Product Strategy, Research, Prototyping, User flows, Design System, Branding, Website, Dashboard.
  • Founding designer and product strategist for a B2B SaaS focused on transforming managers from all sectors into effective leaders.
  • Led user research and product discovery, mapping user needs and translating them into micro-learning flows, team development tools, and leadership maturity diagnostics.
  • Designed intuitive, engaging, and mobile-friendly user interfaces—from climate surveys to daily reflections, feedback systems, and 360º assessments.
  • Developed and maintained a robust front-end in Vue.js, ensuring seamless UX across web and mobile contexts.
  • Co-created Weego’s proprietary methodology to drive leadership development across industries and levels of maturity.
Goals
  • Enable leaders—regardless of sector or experience level—to reflect, develop and act more intentionally with their teams.
  • Design a scalable platform to support daily development habits through nudges, content, and data-driven insights.
  • Create clear, actionable feedback systems and 360º evaluations tailored to the user’s leadership stage.
  • Empower teams through trust, communication, and transparency, using data to drive continuous improvement.
  • Build a culture of growth where leaders at all levels could understand their impact and improve it sustainably.
Process
  • Research & Ideation
    Conducted interviews and surveys with leaders from diverse industries to identify common pain points in team management and personal development.
  • Flow Mapping
    Mapped detailed user journeys for new and experienced leaders, covering scenarios such as onboarding, self-reflection, feedback cycles, and action planning.
  • Wireframes & Specs
    Created wireframes for dashboards, surveys, and learning modules, aligning stakeholders and ensuring usability across leadership levels.
  • Final Design
    Delivered a UI that balanced clarity with motivation—blending microcopy, illustrations, and interactive elements to engage leaders in their journey.
  • Front-end Development
    Coded the front-end architecture using Vue.js and SCSS, creating reusable components and integrating logic for dynamic, personalized experiences.
  • Validation & Iteration
    Ran usability tests and A/B experiments with leaders from different sectors, applying insights to refine features, copy, and UX flows.
Impact & Results
  • Achieved 3x higher engagement than market benchmarks for leadership training platforms.
  • Validated our methodology with HR leaders from top-tier companies, contributing to strategic HR programs nationwide.
  • Increased participation in climate surveys and daily reflection exercises by more than 40%.
  • Built trust in leadership journeys, with 85% of users reporting meaningful improvement in their leadership capabilities.
  • Recognized by HR leaders for providing actionable insights into team engagement, retention, and innovation potential.
