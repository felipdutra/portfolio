Felipe is a 34-yo product designer and front-end developer from São Paulo, Brazil. With over 11 years of experience, he designs and builds digital products end to end, from early research to production-ready interfaces, combining a strong sense of design with solid front-end skills.
With a focus on the space between design and code, Felipe specializes in CSS, design systems, animation, component APIs, and creating interfaces with personality and clarity.
He’s worked across startups and fast-paced teams, often as the sole designer, collaborating closely with founders, engineers, and product people.
